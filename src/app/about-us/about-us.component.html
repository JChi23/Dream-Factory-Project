Who we are

As children, we are all big dreamers. We've all had wishes we hope to be granted from visiting DisneyLand to meeting our heroes. At some point, if we're fortunate enough, we get our wishes granted. However, for children who struggle with critical or chronic illnesses, the road may be too short or difficult for them to see their dreams come true. Our mission is to grant hopeful children with critical illnesses their wishes so that we can bring a little ray of light and hope into their lives.
