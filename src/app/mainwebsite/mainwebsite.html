Granting wishes in St. Louis since 1983

Welcome to the Dream Factory, where children's wishes come true!

For children who don't know what the future holds, we hope to bring hope, relief, and happy memories.

Who we are

Our mission is to grant 100 dreams every year to children between the ages of 3-18 to bring them smiles and memories they hold to their hearts. The help of volunteers and donors is always greatly appreciated.
