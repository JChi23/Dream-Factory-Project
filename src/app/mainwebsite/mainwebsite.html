Welcome to the Dream Factory, where we make children's wishes come true!

Granting Wishes to Children in the St. Louis area since 1983

For children who don't know what the future holds, we hope to bring them hope, relief, and happy memories to keep with them.

We are a volunteer organization aiming to grant dreams to children from the ages of 3-18 with life or threatening chronic illnesses! We hope to grant 100 dreams per year and give children smiles and memories to hold to their hearts. Here are some cool things we've done recently!
